IMPORTANT: This project has been written by Jacques SERIZAY, for the purpose of analysing public datasets.

Today you will be focusing on investigating the ATAC-seq data generated in Janes et al., 2018: “Chromatin accessibility dynamics across C. elegans development and ageing”.
